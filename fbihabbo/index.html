Section (A) General Rules


1) Respect the Habbo Way.

2) Dancing is prohibited within the F.B.I.

3) Use of effects is prohibited within all parts of the F.B.I.

4) Colour chat is reserved for Top Management and above limited to subtle colors. Red should only be used for announcements from SM and above.

5) Spamming chat, or sending the same message multiple times (except for occasional welcome messages -> max 2 per screen) is prohibited.

6) No sentences in full capital letters.

7) Double Jobbing (working in the F.B.I and another agency simultaneously) is prohibited. This includes using 2 separate accounts.

8) Self Promoting (changing your motto to the next rank without a higher rank's permission) is prohibited.

9) Insisting for pay, admins, or a promotion is prohibited. If you believe you deserve any of the above you may ask once politely and with a good reason you should receive the above.

10) Asking for rights is always prohibited and will be met with suspicion.

11) In order to enter the F.B.I you must have the correct badge, motto, and uniform.

12) Going AFK at work stations such as FTF, FTS, the help desk, or the transfer desk is prohibited.

13) Do not troll other agencies or enter other agencies with malice intention.

14) Respect all F.B.I members and guests regardless of rank.

15) Any High Rank member caught ignoring any of these rules or not warning others can result in a warning or demotion.



Section (B) Commands


/FTF/: Fill The Front - Sit at an open recruiting desk at the front of the HQ near the entrance. Proceed to let in members with the correct badge, motto, and uniform. Follow the recruiting policy for new members.

/FTS/: Fill The Security - If you are RSO+ and have been trained to use security, go to the security area and sit at an open desk.

/FTB/: Fill The Back - Go to the AFK seating area to go AFK or wait for a work station to open.

/BTB/: Back To Base - If you are outside of the <F.B.I> base return immediately.

/ATT/: Attention - Stand and face the superior ranking officer that has called ATT, wave and say: “Yes sir/ma’am.”

/AE/: At Ease - Resume what you were doing before being commanded to ATT. During ATT, you are not permitted to return to work before being dismissed.



Section (C) Uniform



Standard Rank Requirement (Ranks before RSO)


All Standard Ranks must wear the FBI provided uniform. There are six uniforms a Standard Rank may wear:

- Standard Male Uniform (Found on mannequins or by using gates)

- Standard Female Uniform (Found on mannequins or by using gates)

- HC Male Uniform (Found on mannequins)

- HC Female Uniform (Found on mannequins

Standard Rank Uniform Guidelines


- Non distracting hats are permitted, however, if they are unnecessary or unprofessional they must be taken off.

- Simple glasses are permitted but flashy or unnecessary glasses are not allowed.

- Facial items should not cover entire face. Distracting items must be taken off.

- Unrealistic skin colours like red, green, blue, etc. are prohibited.

- No uniform changes are allowed.

Trainer & LCU Rank Uniform Guidelines


- Dress professionally with solid dark colours (white is allowed).

- Do not wear unnecessary head items.

HR - OOA Uniform Guidelines


- Dress Professionally

- Shirts must be worn at all times (bikini tops and bare chests - on males - are not permitted);

- Pants must be worn at all times (bikini bottoms are prohibited).

- Glasses are allowed, however, masks of any kind are prohibited.

- Flashy, distracting, or generally unprofessional clothing items are prohibited.

JM Ranks and above


The highest ranks of the F.B.I, JM+, are expected to be models for the F.B.I and lower ranks. They must dress professionally.

- Masks are allowed.

- Bikini tops/bare chest and bikini bottoms are prohibited.

- As a top staff member you are at own risk of dressing inappropriately.



Section (D) Transfer Requirements


Because of our agency's size (85,000+) not all transfers will keep a rank similar to their rank in their old agency. If your agency is considerably smaller your rank will be lower.

- You may join the Transfer Unit if you have been in the F.B.I for more than 2 weeks and are OOA+.

- You must have at least 500 people on your main badge to transfer.

- You must have been at your previous agency for 5+ days and provide a reason you are leaving.

- Even friends are to be given a rank according to the rules stated on the policy.

Rank based on agency size


- Agency: 500 - 2000 /: Receives a few rank groups lower

- Agency 2000 - 5000 /: Receives a rank group or two lower

- Agency 5000+ /: Receives similar rank unless the agency faces inactivity!

Transfer Mentor Program


- After you transfer you must keep track of the person you transferred. Give them a tour of the HQ, make sure they know all the commands, and show them how to use security. If they have any questions they should come to you.

- If you do not help the transferee out after transferring you may have your TU badge removed.



Section (E) Discipline



Small rule breaking such as disrespect, dancing in the HQ, using effects, etc. should be met with a verbal warning, then, if they continue, they should be demoted.

They should only be striked after they have continuously been warned & demoted.

Firing on the spot


If an <F.B.I> member shows no regards to be part of the F.B.I, trolls, does not change motto when is told to, and doesn't listen to any authority you should not waste time on the striking process- they should be fired on the spot.

- Standard Ranks can be fired on the spot by PS+

- RSO - LCU can be fired on the spot by SLC+

- HR - SLC may be fired on the spot by TM+

- OOA may be fired on the spot by Joint Owner+

- JM+ may not be fired on the spot.



Section (F) Promoting & Training



When to promote


You should promote when you believe a member has been working hard for more than the suggested promotion time. If they go beyond what is expected of them they should be promoted. After a certain amount of time, effort, and good work is done a worker should be promoted.

Approximate time between promotions



- Standard Rank: 20 - 30 minutes

- RSO: ~35 minutes

- Trainers: ~45 minutes

- LCU: ~50 minutes

- HR: ~60 minutes

- PS: ~1.5 hours

- SDU: Max 3 times a day, ~2 hours

- IOB: Max 3 times a day, ~2 hours 30 mins

- SLC: Max twice a day, ~4 hours

- OOA: Max twice a day ~5 hours

- JM: Weekly (considering activity)

Promotion/Demotion Logging


- Every promotion/demotion must be logged

- A recruit that has been trained must be logged too

Promotion/Demotion Guidelines


- You must be at least LCU to promote.

- You must be at least HR to demote.

- For ranks up to SLC you must be 10 ranks above the rank you are promoting into. You must be 20 ranks above the rank the person starts with to demote.

- For SLC you must be OOA+ and 20 ranks above the rank you are promoting into. You must be OOA+ and 30 ranks above the rank the person starts with to demote.

- OOA may only be promoted by JM+. They may only be demoted by SM+.

- JM can promote/demote anyone up to the top 10 ranks of OOA.

- SM can promote/demote anyone up to the top rank of OOA.

- EOs into JM and up to the last rank of JM.

- COs and AOs may promote up to SM (Economic Leader).

- JOs may promote into SM and up to CEO.

- Dir. Reps are to discuss promotions/demotions higher than SM with the Director.

- Only Directorate may promote into TM, promote/demote within TM, or demote out of TM.

Section (H) Emergency Procedures


In this section, you will learn to deal with issues that may occur in HQ. In the result of these situations, it is important not to panic. Higher ranks have dealt with this before and can deal with it again.

Please use the following advice to aid you:

1) Raiders or Trollers


- Place the offending user in ignore by clicking on their avatar and then ‘ignore’;

- Do not let them into HQ;

- Contact a RR holder and alert them of the situation;

- If you are a HR, go ahead and reassure your lower ranks and notify them to ignore the troller if this has not been done already. It is as much your responsibility as it is theirs to respond to the situation accordingly.

- Screenshot if possible.

2) Trollers Inside HQ


- Contact a RR holder immediately to kick or ban the troller(s);

- Ensure all FTF seats are occupied by workers;

- If you are a HR, go ahead and reassure your lower ranks and notify them to ignore the troller if this has not been done already. It is as much your responsibility as it is theirs to respond to the situation accordingly.

- Screenshot if possible.

3) Inactivity & Leave of Absence


The higher rank you are, the less time you should take away from Habbo. Standard ranks are not included in time taken away and can keep their rank indefinitely. Inactive without notice:

- OOA: badge removed after 14 days of inactivity.

- JM: demoted after 5 days, JM badge removed after 7 days.

- SM: demoted after 4 days, SM badge removed after 7 days.

- TM: demoted after 3 days, TM badge removed after 5 days.

Demoting/removing someone without logging can go against your own favour, do not forget!
Leave of Absence is to be announced max a day before you leave.
Get a TM+ member’s approval, if you are TM yourself you should speak to one of the Directorate members. If you were unexpectedly unable to come online (e.g. internet cut) and were not able to contact a TM member, you can discuss this with a TM member or Director. If you are a TM member yourself, you can discuss this with a Director.

Deputy Director

Unit Overseer (Max 3x per Unit):

Unit Operative
Min Rank - HR
Role - Operatives are to follow the instruction of the Directors and produce results with the guidance .

Rules and Regs:


- New Special Units cost 25c. To start a Unit, you must publish a document explaining how the SU will run detailing the use and benefit to the FBI. All new units must first be approved by the CoS and DCoS.

- Any SU suspected to have malicious goals or ideas will be shut down immediately

- No SUs have the power to fire their Co-Workers, if they believe one of them have committed a serious offense please bring it to Directorate and it will be dealt with.

- No SU has more power than another, all SUs will in fact have no power, but the responsibility to carry out certain jobs given to them.

- No SU can collect money from FBI staff, all financial requirements will be met by Directorate.

- Any classified information, confidential within the SU shall not be leaked, doing so may result in removal from your SU.

- All SU badges must be owned by Harry6.

For more Special Unit information, visit the SU page.

Alliances

The Federal Bureau of Investigation is constantly looking forward to forging strong alliances with both new and old agencies. Potential allies would need to fulfil our minimum requirements before being considered. If you have any further enquiries, please feel free to approach our External Affairs team!

Requirements and Terms of Alliance

Minimum requirements to be considered:

  • A minimum of 1,000 members on the main badge.
  • An active and stable Foundation/Directorate team and Head Quarters.
  • Be in agreement of the Federal Bureau of Investigation's Terms of Alliance.
  • Be in agreement to the Federal Bureau of Investigation's National Punishment Policy.

The Federal Bureau of Investigation's Terms of Alliance:

  • To maintain a strong and stable relationship with the Federal Bureau of Investigation and its members.
  • Plotting against the Federal Bureau of Investigation is strongly prohibited.
  • No raiding/trolling/disrespecting the Federal Bureau of Investigation and its members.
  • To agree upon a suitable alliance agreement between both agencies.
